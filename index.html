PLACES JAYLA WANTS TO TRAVEL

Jayla would honestyly travel anywhere. I love tropical climates, I loves snowy, col, winter places. I love fall when the leaves are falling.



Bora Bora

I would want to go here because it is literally just perfect. I want to stay in a water bungalow so that I can swim whenever, wherever I want. Bora Bora is a popular destination for honeymoons and luxury travel but when I go I'll be getting to the water and stay there forever. There are activities like snorkeling, scuba diving with sharks, and relaxing on white sand beaches drinking unlimited pina coladas.

Switzerland

Switzerland is such a naturally beautiful place. I would go buy some rich, delicious chocolates and just listen to my music and stare at the beautiful mountains and scenery, watch the sunset and stare at the stars.

Thailand

Thailand is a Southeast Asian country. It's known for tropical beaches, opulent royal palaces, ancient ruins and ornate temples displaying figures of Buddha. You get a mix of city, culture, beaches and all the best things in one.
